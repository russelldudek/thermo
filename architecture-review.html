One-page decision worksheet

Does this capability earn the right to scale?

Make outcome, data authority, AI role, human authority, controls, integration, operations, economics, adoption, and reuse explicit before promotion.

1. Outcome & owner

What decision or workflow changes? Who owns the outcome and baseline?

2. Data authority

Which sources are authoritative? What are lineage, quality, access, and retention boundaries?

3. AI role & evidence

What does the model, RAG system, or agent do? What evaluation and grounding evidence is required?

4. Human authority

Which decisions remain human? Where must the system abstain, escalate, or allow override?

5. Integration & exceptions

Which APIs, events, systems, and RPA steps are involved? How are failure and exceptions handled?

6. Trust & control

What privacy, security, compliance, threat, and review controls apply?

7. Operations & economics

Who supports it? What are SLOs, incident path, rollback, unit cost, and value measures?

8. Adoption, reuse & disposition

How will retained use be measured? Promote, revise, hold, retire, or exempt - and why?

Final disposition
